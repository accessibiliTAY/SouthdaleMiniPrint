About Us

​Southdale Miniprint is a family owned and operated business centered around our local roots and dedication to our product. Founded by my father in 1969, we have continued the business legacy through hard work and keeping up with the latest trends. Here at Southdale MiniPrint we offer full service printing. Not sure what that means? In essence, you can come to us with an idea and we can make it come to life through every step from conception, design, and the final product. ​Colors and design are created through Pantone Color Codes, allowing you to chose from an array of vibrant and true-toned colors. Southdale Miniprint can satisfy your digital and offset printing needs.
